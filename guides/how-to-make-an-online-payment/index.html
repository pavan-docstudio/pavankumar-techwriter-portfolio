How to make an online payment

info

About this sample: This guide demonstrates a standard financial transaction flow (Mobile Top-up). Similar logic applies to B2C checkout processes in e-commerce.

Goal: Help users complete a payment with zero errors.

Result: The documentation met all requirements and compliance standards, resulting in a job offer from a major IT integrator.

Evolution: I create the original content as a time-boxed test task. Later, I refined and edited the text to align with STE principles for better clarity and conciseness.

With this user guide, you learn how to make an online payment.

As a reference, we use the process of making a mobile top-up in the Credo Bank mobile app.

Step 1: Choose the "Money in/out" option​

After logging in to the Credo Bank mobile app, click on Money in/out.

Step 2: Choose the "Mobile TopUp" option​

In the Money in/out page, click on Mobile TopUp.

Step 3: Enter the mobile number​

In the Mobile TopUp page, find the necessary mobile number. Enter the number manually or select it from your contacts. Then, click the Next button.

Step 4: Enter the top-up amount​

In the checkout page, write the necessary amount in the Amount to Pay field.

Step 5: Select a necessary account​

Then, in the same page, click Select Account.

Step 6: Choose an account and a currency​

In the accounts page, click on your necessary account. Select the currency.

Step 7: Make a payment​

After choosing the account, you return to the checkout page. To make a payment, check the data and click the Pay button.

Step 8: Receive a confirmation​

After the payment, the confirmation page opens.

note

The year of writing is 2025.
